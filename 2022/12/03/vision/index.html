Dear friends：

In today’s world, all countries are faced with various economic problems. Because of the non renewable resources, there are various contradictions. Because of the uneven economic development, there are various frictions. Because of the massive information output every day, we gradually can’t do anything to find the source of information from unknown sources and do not know whether the Tao should believe it.

Because unreliable information slowly forms an isolated and closed society, which makes our work, entrepreneurship and financing difficult, and makes young people at a loss when they are developing innovation and entrepreneurship. Therefore, we hope to establish an enhancement tool for productivity enhancement and protection, to scientifically measure and allocate resources, to dynamically try to alleviate or even solve various conflicts and disputes.

My vision is to build a society with equal opportunities, mutual trust, harmony and prosperity. Every poor person has the opportunity to use what he has learned from books to make scientific judgments so as to gain wealth. He does not have to be servile to pursue the life he wants.

I hope that every young person can get the same treatment and income without new class antagonism due to various educational qualifications or qualification thresholds.

The products that farmers grow by themselves can be sold by themselves without resorting to anyone.

Workers can sell their products through the platform to obtain part of the profits, without having to ask the sales staff.

So that businessmen can obtain controllable risk investment strategies and returns based on macroeconomic information such as economic market, policy, population, etc.

So that young people can cultivate their own artificial intelligence systems to explore the unknown universe without having to struggle for a living.

Let researchers share their achievements and earn income.
